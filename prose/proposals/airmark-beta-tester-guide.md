# Welcome to the AirMark Beta Program
### How Your Feedback Shapes AirMark

---

Hey Beta Testers!

First off — thank you. Being part of the AirMark beta program means you're not just a user, you're a co-builder. Everything you tell us directly influences what we build next, what we fix first. This document walks you through exactly how to give us feedback and what happens after you do.

---

## How to Give Us Feedback

It couldn't be simpler. While you're using AirMark, you'll see a **"Give Feedback"** button in the app. Click it anytime something feels off, something is broken, or you have an idea for something that would make your life easier.

A small panel will pop up right inside the app — you never have to leave what you're doing. Just:

1. **Pick a type** — is it a bug, a feature idea, or a general UX frustration?
2. **Give it a title** — one quick sentence describing it
3. **Describe it** — a little more detail about what happened or what you'd love to see
4. **Hit Submit** — that's it, you're done

We automatically capture your browser and device info in the background so you never have to write "I'm on Chrome on Windows" — we already know.

---

## What Happens After You Submit

This is the part most feedback tools get wrong — you submit something and it disappears into a black hole. Not here. Here's exactly what happens after you hit submit:

```
You submit feedback inside AirMark
        ↓
It instantly lands on our team's radar
        ↓
Our product team reviews it within the week
        ↓
If it makes the cut → it goes into our development sprint
        ↓
Our devs build it or fix it
        ↓
We let you know when it ships
```

Your feedback doesn't sit in a spreadsheet collecting dust. It goes directly into the same system our developers use to plan and build AirMark — so the path from your idea to a real feature is as short as possible.

---

## What Kind of Feedback Is Most Helpful

All feedback is welcome, but here's what moves the needle most for us:

** Bugs** — Something isn't working the way it should. Be specific! "The export button on the Dashboard doesn't respond when I click it in Firefox" is great feedback for us.

** Feature Requests** — Something you wish AirMark could do that it can't yet. Frame it around your workflow: *"I wish I could do X so that I don't have to Y."* That context helps us understand the real problem, not just the surface request.

** UX Frustrations** — Something that works technically but feels clunky, confusing, or slow. These are gold for us. If something makes you sigh every time you do it — tell us. 

---

## A Few Things to Know

- **You don't need any new accounts** — feedback happens right inside AirMark, nothing extra required
- **No feedback is too small** — a one-sentence annoyance is just as valuable as a detailed feature spec
- **You won't be bothered constantly** — we're not going to flood your inbox. We'll reach out when something you flagged ships, or occasionally to ask a follow-up question
- **Your feedback is private** — only the AirMark team sees what you submit
